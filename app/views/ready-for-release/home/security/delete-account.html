Are you sure you want to delete your GOV.UK One Login?

This will permanently delete the sign in details for your GOV.UK One Login.

You will no longer be able to use your GOV.UK One Login to access:

  • Sign your mortgage deed
  • Request a basic DBS check
  • Check your State Pension forecast

It will not delete the personal information held by services you’ve used.

If you need a GOV.UK One Login in the future, you’ll have to create a new one.

Deleting your GOV.UK One Login will not affect any other government accounts you may have, such as Government Gateway or Universal Credit.
